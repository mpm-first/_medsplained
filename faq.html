Frequently Asked Questions

This is where you should answer the most common questions prospective customers might have. It’s a good idea to cover things like your return policy, product warranty info, shipping and returns, etc. Check out the examples below.

Q.

What exactly does MedSplained do for me?

A.

We at MedSplained are here to offer you timely access to specialized doctors whose sole focus is to listen, answer questions and help you better understand your health or the health of a loved one. Our ultimate goal is to put you in the driver’s seat of your healthcare, with us helping you navigate along the way.

Q.

Is MedSplained any different from the information I can find on the internet?

A.

Yes! We are proud to provide you and your family with real-time and highly personalized explanations, summarized in a way that makes sense for you and your life. Instead of spending hours searching the internet trying to discern fact from opinion, you have access to best and brightest resident physicians in the country who give the most up-to-date and evidence-based information with clear answers you can trust.

Q.

Will MedSplained be able to diagnose me?

A.

MedSplained is a service that aims to help you understand more about your health or the health of a loved one through personalized education. We do not try (and really shouldn’t) give a diagnosis or conclusion on a specific problem or condition. This is best done by your treating physician (primary care provider, specialist or surgeon) who has the opportunity to know your full medical history, examine you and prescribe treatment. Our job is to help you understand elements of your care. We are here to listen and answer any questions you might have, explain all the options regarding your care and make sure you feel confident in owning your healthcare going forward. Our goal is not to diagnose or treat you, but to put you in the driver’s seat of your healthcare so that you and your doctor together can make the best decision for your health and your life.

Q.

Will MedSplained offer any medical advice or treatment?

A.

While we will meet you where-ever you are along your health journey, MedSplained cannot offer medical advice or treatment. This is best done by the doctor or healthcare provider who take care of you, knows you best and has examined you. We are here to be your health partner and offer you all the information available regarding your condition or proposed treatment. We put you in touch with specialized doctors who can explain everything in such a way that you will be able to take ownership over your healthcare needs. This empowers you to make the best plan with your doctor about next steps.

Q.

Can I ask questions on behalf of a loved one who I am concerned about?

A.

Absolutely! We welcome clients to call and inquire about healthcare questions for family or for loved ones. Even if you don’t know all the details of their care, we can help provide general information or even clarifying questions to share with their doctor.

Q.

How often can I reach out to MedSplained?

A.

We are available at all times and we hope you contact us a much as you need to feel comfortable with the information presented. Our goal is to partner with you and empower you to be in the driver’s seat of your care or help a loved one’s with the understanding of their healthcare needs.
